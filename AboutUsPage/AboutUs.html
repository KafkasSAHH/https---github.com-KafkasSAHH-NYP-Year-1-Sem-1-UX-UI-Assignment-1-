ABOUT NYP BADMINTON

Our Badminton Journey

Welcome to NYP's Badminton CCA - where passion meets precision!

Our team has been smashing our way to success since 2010, cultivating both competitive players and recreational enthusiasts. Whether you're looking to compete at national levels or just want to enjoy friendly matches, our doors are open to all who share a love for this fast-paced sport.

Our Training Philosophy

We believe in developing well-rounded players through rigorous training that focuses on technique, strategy, and physical conditioning. Our weekly sessions include:

  • Footwork drills for agility and speed
  • Smash practice for powerful attacks
  • Doubles positioning and teamwork
  • Match simulations for real-game experience

Beyond skills, we emphasize sportsmanship, teamwork, and the never-give-up spirit that defines true badminton champions.

Our Achievements

In recent years, our team has proudly represented NYP in various inter-polytechnic competitions, bringing home several medals in both singles and doubles categories. Our players have also been selected for national youth training squads.

But beyond trophies, we measure success by the friendships formed and personal growth achieved through the sport.
