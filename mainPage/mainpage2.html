Join Our Badminton CCA!

Badminton is more than just a sport - it's about teamwork, discipline, and fun! Our CCA welcomes players of all skill levels, whether you're a beginner or an experienced player.

Why Join Us?

  • Improve your badminton skills with regular training sessions
  • Make new friends who share your passion for the sport
  • Represent the school in competitions
  • Stay active and healthy
  • Learn valuable teamwork and leadership skills
"Being part of the Badminton CCA has helped me grow both as a player and a leader. The camaraderie and friendly competition make every session enjoyable!" - Xie Ming En, Captain
"Even if you've never played before, don't worry! We'll help you learn and improve while having fun." - Melvin Lim, Vice Captain
